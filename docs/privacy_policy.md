- **Account and profile information** you enter during setup (such as name, email address, and GitHub username).

## 3. Data Handling and Sharing

Gimble stores configuration locally on your machine. When cloud features are enabled, selected information and configuration data may be transmitted to Gimble Cloud to enable features such as remote access, telemetry, or account verification. Data may also be transmitted to third-party providers that you configure, such as model APIs.
